## ðŸ‘¤ Operators & Cosmetics

Operators are the playable characters in the game. While they do not have unique abilities, they can be customized with a variety of cosmetic items.

- **Operator Skins:** Full-body outfits for operators.
- **Weapon Blueprints:** Custom-configured weapons with unique appearances.
- **Emblems & Calling Cards:** Customizable player profile icons.

See [Assets_Operators.md](Assets_Operators.md) for more information.
